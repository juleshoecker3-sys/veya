How fast will my order ship?+

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days and you'll get a tracking link by email. Standard US delivery is typically 3–7 business days. Shipping is free on orders over $70.

Can I take the Focus and Sleep products together?+

Yes — they're designed for opposite ends of your day. Take a Focus product (Brain & Focus Formula or Focus Powder) in the morning, and a Sleep product (Sleep Support, Sleep Formula, or Sleep Strips) 30–60 minutes before bed.

Will the Focus products keep me up at night?+

Our Focus range is built around clean, steady energy — Focus Powder uses L-theanine alongside natural caffeine to smooth out the jitters and crash. Take it earlier in the day and it won't interfere with your sleep.

Are the Sleep products habit-forming?+

No. Our Sleep range uses low-dose, naturally-derived ingredients — magnesium, botanicals, and a measured amount of melatonin — to work with your own sleep cycle. They're non-habit forming.

What's your return policy?+

Do you ship internationally?+

Yes — we ship to most countries via USPS and DHL, with rates and delivery times calculated at checkout based on your destination.

Are your supplements third-party tested?+

Yes. Every formula is made in the USA and third-party tested for purity and potency, with full ingredient transparency — no proprietary blends.
