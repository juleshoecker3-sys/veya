Our Story

We were tired of choosing.

Wired all day, then wide awake at night. LUCID started with one simple refusal: that you shouldn't have to trade your focus for your rest.

The supplement aisle is split in two. One shelf promises to switch you on — harder, faster, more. The other promises to knock you out. Both treat your mind like a switch, and neither treats it like yours.

LUCID began in 2024, in the gap between those two shelves. Our founder was running on a familiar loop: a stack of caffeine and pre-workout to get through the day, then a melatonin gummy and a prayer to get through the night. The energy was borrowed. The sleep was groggy. And the middle — the actual clear-headed hours — kept getting thinner.

So we asked a different question. Not "what makes you wired?" or "what knocks you out?" — but what does a genuinely clear mind actually need across a full 24 hours? The answer wasn't one product. It was two, designed to work as a rhythm: sharpen what's worth sharpening by day, and truly recover by night.

"We don't sell energy or sleep. We sell clarity — the kind that lasts all day and lets go at night."

We spent eighteen months with formulators and the actual research — not marketing claims. We threw out anything dosed below what studies support, anything we couldn't pronounce, and any "proprietary blend" that hid what was inside. What survived became LUCID Focus and LUCID Rest: clinically-dosed, fully transparent, third-party tested, and built to be taken every day without dependence.
